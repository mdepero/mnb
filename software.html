Pre-Installed Software

Whether you choose an Apple or Dell laptop, the software you need to connect to Miami's wired and wireless network comes pre-installed on your Miami notebook. So do all the little things that make it easier to use your laptop at Miami.

  • LibreOffice provides functionality similar to Microsoft Office. While not as full featured as the commercial office product, LibreOffice is free and meets many of the basic needs of a student for writing papers and preparing presentations.
  • Microsoft Security Essentials protects your computer from viruses and spyware, two of the leading threats to computers connected to the Internet. This software is configured to download updates weekly so your computer stays protected from new threats as they emerge.
  • Cisco AnyConnect Virtual Private Network (VPN) client establishes a secure connection to Miami's on-campus network from off-campus locations, such as your home, an off-campus apartment, or a coffee shop.
  • Drive Mapping Tool mounts network resources used by students to store and retrieve coursework, to turn in assignments, to back-up important files, and to publish web-pages.
  • uPrint allows you to select a public printer in locations across campus, such as King Library, for printing and copying.
  • Bomgar Shortcut allows quick access to ITHelp for technology questions or for troubleshooting a problem on your computer.
  • And more... popular free software utilities – like FireFox, Adobe Acrobat Reader and iTunes– are also pre-loaded to save you time and supply the tools you need to be productive in and out of the classroom.

Optional Software

The Miami University Bookstore offers software at an educational discount and many software titles are available for special order.

  • Microsoft Office is the standard at Miami for business applications. Office for Windows includes Word, Excel, Powerpoint, Outlook, Access and Publisher. Office for Mac includes Word, Excel, Powerpoint and Outlook.
  • Adobe Creative Suite Standard includes Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign and Acrobat Pro. Creative Suite Premium includes Photoshop Extended, Illustrator, InDesign,Acrobat Pro, Dreamweaver, Flash and Fireworks.
  • And more... Many titles are available for special order through the Miami University Bookstore. These include EndNote, Dragon dictate, Adobe Lightroom, and many others.
